ethiochicken

Ethiochicken is a leading poultry company in Ethiopia, dedicated to improving the livelihoods of smallholder farmers through the provision of high-quality poultry products and services.

For more information, you can visit their official website: Ethiochicken

Ethiochicken is committed to empowering Ethiopian farmers by providing them with access to quality poultry breeds, feed, and veterinary services. Their mission is to enhance food security and create sustainable livelihoods in rural communities.

Ethiochicken's innovative approach includes training programs for farmers, ensuring they have the knowledge and skills to raise poultry effectively. They also focus on improving the overall poultry value chain in Ethiopia.

With a strong emphasis on quality and sustainability, Ethiochicken plays a crucial role in the Ethiopian poultry industry, contributing to economic growth and rural development.
